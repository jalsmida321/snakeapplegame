About The Game

In Pacman Unblocked, you control the hungry yellow character who must navigate a maze while eating dots and avoiding colorful ghosts. The objective is simple: clear the maze by eating all the dots while dodging the ghosts who are out to get you.

As you advance through the levels, the maze becomes more complex, and the ghosts become faster and smarter. You can eat power pellets to turn the tables, making the ghosts vulnerable for a short time. The game also features fruit items that appear in the maze, offering extra points when eaten.

How to Play

  1. Use arrow keys to control Pac-Man's movement through the maze
  2. Eat all the dots to complete each level
  3. Avoid the colorful ghosts that chase you through the maze
  4. Collect power pellets to temporarily turn the tables on ghosts
  5. Eat fruit items for bonus points
  6. Clear all dots to advance to more challenging levels
