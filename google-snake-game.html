Operation Instructions

On January 29, 2013, Google unveiled the Snake game, also referred to as Google Matopeli, as a Google doodle to commemorate the Chinese New Year. The doodle converted the Google logo into a playable snake game, challenging players to maneuver the snake to consume food while avoiding self-collisions or hitting the walls. With each food item consumed, the snake's length increased, enhancing the difficulty. This uncomplicated yet engaging design quickly won the hearts of Google users.Click the link to experience it:https://elgoog.im/snake/
