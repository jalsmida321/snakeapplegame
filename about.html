About the publication

Browser games, clearly presented.

SnakeAppleGa.me is an independent browser-game website built to make quick play easy and to publish useful, plain-English context around the games people enjoy.

By SnakeAppleGa.me Editorial Team

What we publish

We organize games that run in a web browser, including snake games, puzzles, arcade games, and other casual titles. Our pages may include instructions, gameplay context, selection notes, and links that help visitors find another game.

Some games are hosted or built by third parties and appear through embedded frames. An embed allows a game to run inside a SnakeAppleGa.me page, but it does not make us the developer, publisher, or owner of that game.

Our editorial standards

The SnakeAppleGa.me Editorial Team aims to keep descriptions accurate, useful, and easy to understand. We distinguish our own editorial text from third-party game content, avoid presenting speculation as fact, and review material when a credible concern is raised.

Commercial considerations do not excuse inaccurate descriptions. Advertising or partnership discussions do not guarantee coverage, placement, or a favorable opinion.

Corrections and updates

Games, embeds, and external services change. If a page contains a factual error, broken game, incorrect credit, or misleading description, email jalsmida@gmail.com with the page URL, the issue, and supporting information. We review specific, verifiable reports and update content when warranted.

For copyright or takedown concerns, include enough information to identify the work and the exact material at issue. See the Contact page for the most useful details.

Independence and trademarks

SnakeAppleGa.me is an independent website. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or operated by Google, Nokia, or any other game developer, platform, brand, or trademark owner unless a page explicitly says otherwise.
