Privacy, without the maze.

This policy explains what information may be processed when you visit SnakeAppleGa.me, play an embedded game, view the leaderboard, or contact us.

Last updated July 21, 2026

Who operates this site

SnakeAppleGa.me is operated under the displayed identity SnakeAppleGa.me Editorial Team. Privacy questions can be sent to jalsmida@gmail.com.

Information processed when you visit

Like most websites, SnakeAppleGa.me and its infrastructure providers may process technical request data such as your IP address, browser and device type, operating system, referring page, requested pages, timestamps, and security or diagnostic events. This information is used to deliver the site, maintain reliability, understand traffic, and prevent abuse.

If you email us, we receive the address you use and the contents and metadata of your message.

Google Analytics 4

The site uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand aggregate usage, such as pages viewed, approximate location derived from network information, device and browser characteristics, referral source, and interactions with the site. GA4 may use cookies or similar technologies and sends analytics data to Google. Google processes that data under its own terms and privacy practices.

Google AdSense and cookies

SnakeAppleGa.me uses Google AdSense to display advertising on some pages. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, and similar technologies to deliver, measure, limit repetition of, and personalize ads, depending on your settings, consent choices, and region. Advertising providers may recognize your browser across websites.

You can manage Google ad personalization through your Google advertising settings and can control cookies through your browser. Blocking advertising cookies may make ads less relevant but should not prevent access to ordinary editorial pages.

Cloudflare and content delivery

Cloudflare helps deliver and protect the website. In doing so, Cloudflare may process network and device information, request logs, and security signals needed to route traffic, cache content, detect malicious activity, and maintain service performance.

Game artwork and other images may be delivered from Cloudflare R2 or other content delivery network (CDN) addresses. Those services receive ordinary request information, including your IP address and browser headers, when your browser requests a file.

Third-party game iframes

Some games are loaded in third-party iframes. When an iframe loads, your browser connects directly to that provider. The provider may receive technical request data and may use cookies, local storage, analytics, advertising, or other technologies under its own privacy policy. SnakeAppleGa.me does not control the provider's code or data practices.

You can choose not to load or interact with an embedded game, and browser privacy tools may let you block third-party cookies or site data. Doing so can cause some games to stop working.

Leaderboard and Google Sheets

The leaderboard is displayed through an embedded Google Sheets document. Viewing it connects your browser to Google, which may process technical and usage information under Google's privacy practices. Leaderboard content may include player names or aliases, scores, and related record details made available in the source sheet. Do not use a sensitive identifier as a player name.

Questions about displayed leaderboard information can be sent to jalsmida@gmail.com.

Local storage

SnakeAppleGa.me pages or games may use your browser's localStorage or similar on-device storage to remember preferences, game state, progress, or scores. This data generally stays in the browser profile where it was created unless the page or embedded provider transmits it. You can remove it using your browser's site-data controls; clearing it may reset saved settings or progress.

External links

Our pages link to websites and services we do not operate. Following an external link allows that destination to collect information according to its own policies. A link does not mean that SnakeAppleGa.me controls or endorses the destination's privacy practices.

Children's privacy

SnakeAppleGa.me is a general-audience website and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly ask children under 13 to submit personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and, where appropriate, delete it. Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of online games, advertising, and third-party services.

Your choices

  • Use browser controls to block or delete cookies and local storage.
  • Use available consent controls and Google settings to manage personalized advertising.
  • Use browser tracking protection or content blocking, understanding that embeds may not work.
  • Avoid interacting with third-party games or external links.
  • Contact us to ask about personal information you sent directly to the SnakeAppleGa.me Editorial Team or to request a correction or deletion where applicable.

Retention

We retain direct correspondence and site security or operational information only for as long as reasonably needed for the purpose for which it was collected, including responding to requests, maintaining the site, preventing abuse, and meeting applicable obligations. Retention periods vary with the type and purpose of the information.

Google, Cloudflare, iframe providers, and other third parties set their own retention practices. Browser-stored data remains until it expires, a game removes it, or you clear it.

Policy changes and contact

We may update this policy when the site, its service providers, or relevant practices change. The effective and last-updated dates at the top identify the current version.

For privacy questions or requests, email the SnakeAppleGa.me Editorial Team at jalsmida@gmail.com. Please include enough detail to identify the page or interaction involved, but do not send passwords or sensitive financial information.

The short version.

We use infrastructure, analytics, ads, embeds, and browser storage to run and understand the site.

Third-party services process data under their own policies.
